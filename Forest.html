About Us

we cannot afford to fail in our mission to save a living planet

Focused on natural foods businesses concerned about climate change, the Climate Collaborative breaks climate action down into three simple steps: commit, act, impact. To date, many businesses have made commitments, and the Climate Collaborative offers a variety of resources and support to these businesses to help them reach their goals.

Climate services (CS) may be defined as scientifically based information and products that enhance users' knowledge and understanding about the impacts of climate on their decisions and actions
